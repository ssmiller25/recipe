A light snack, or great options for some relaxation.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz cheese (normal 3oz cheese)  Types of Cheese
  - Shredded Cheddar
  - Shredded Provolone
  - Shredded Mexican Mix
- Chips - enough to fill a place
- Sides
  - Salsa
  - Sour cream
  - Sarah Mix
    - Salsa - 100g
    - Sour Cream - 60g

## Instructions

1. Spread chips out.
2. Sprinkle cheese evenly
3. Microwave for 20 seconds
4. Enjoy
