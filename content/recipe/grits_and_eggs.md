## Ingredients

- 4-6 eggs
- 1 cup of quick grits
- 4-6 slices of bacon
- 4-6 slices of bread

## Instructions

1. Make quick grits according to directions.
2. Fry up eggs over-easy
3. Cook bacon
4. Toast bread
