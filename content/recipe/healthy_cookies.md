## Ingredients

- 3 mashed bananas (ripe)
- 1/3 cup apple sauce
- 2 cups oats
- 1/4 cup almond milk
- 1/4 cup raisins or nuts
- 1 cup good dark chocolate chunks (Lilyâ€™s brand is my fave)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Mix all ingredients together
3. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
