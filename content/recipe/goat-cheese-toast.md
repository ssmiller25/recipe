## Ingredients

- Bread
- Goat Cheese
- Tomatoes
- Basil Leaves
- Balsamic Vinegar

## Instructions

1. Toast bread and slice tomatoes
2. Top toast with goat cheese and sliced tomatoes
3. Sprinkle basil leaves
4. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar
